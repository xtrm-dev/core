## Summary

Landed the xtrm-pcon skills tier architecture (the largest single change in the project's history: 183 files, 22k insertions) alongside .mcp.json wiring restoration, then cleaned up the issue board by closing 12 superseded/duplicate beads. Specialist orchestration was used heavily for parallel verification — type safety audit, test coverage gaps, and MCP root cause debugging ran as concurrent waves.
